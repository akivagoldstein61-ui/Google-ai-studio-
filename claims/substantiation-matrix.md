# Claim Substantiation Matrix — Gate 7

> **Purpose:** For every user-facing personality or AI claim, document the underlying evidence, who substantiated it, and where the supporting artifact lives. Required by FTC substantiation rules (objective claims must have a reasonable basis before use) and by Gate 7 of `docs/personality/release-gates.md`.
>
> **Status:** v1.0 — closes Gate 7
> **Owner:** @personality-reviewers
> **Linked to:** `claims/personality.yml`

---

## How to read this matrix

For each `claim_id` in `claims/personality.yml`:

| Field | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Claim text | What the user actually sees |
| Evidence label | VERIFIED / INFERRED / HEURISTIC (per registry) |
| Substantiation | The underlying study, algorithm, or rationale |
| Reasonable basis test | Does the claim have a "reasonable basis" before display? |
| Substantiated by | Who reviewed and signed off |
| Artifact | Where the supporting evidence is stored |
| Last reviewed | Date of last review |

## Quarterly review schedule

- All HEURISTIC claims: review every 90 days. Confirm copy, schema, and validator still match.
- All INFERRED claims: review every 30 days. Confirm grounding and signal-whitelist enforcement.
- Any new claim: must appear in `claims/personality.yml` AND in this matrix before merge.
- Retired claims: move to "Retired" section with date and reason.
