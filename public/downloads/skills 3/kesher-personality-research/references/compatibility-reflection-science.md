## Research Boundary

- `VERIFIED`: Lower neuroticism or higher emotional stability, agreeableness, conscientiousness, attachment security, dyadic coping, and responsive interaction are relationship-relevant patterns.
- `VERIFIED`: Actor effects are often more dependable than claims about a particular pair's destiny.
- `VERIFIED`: Similarity exists in pairing patterns, but its value for established relationship quality is mixed and often overstated.
- `INFERRED`: Kesher should translate these patterns into reflection prompts, conversation questions, and micro-habits rather than pair verdicts.

## Allowed Product Surfaces

- Private owner reflection cards.
- Mutual-consent compatibility reflection after both users opt in.
- Question prompts that help users discuss values, communication, conflict repair, and pacing.
- Non-numeric "possible strength" and "possible friction" language.

## Blocked Product Surfaces

- Numeric compatibility or match percentages.
- Soulmate, destiny, or perfect-pair language.
- Advice to pursue or avoid a specific person based on personality.
- Public trait ranks or cross-user trait comparisons.
- Hidden personality-driven discovery explanations.

## Output Shape

Prefer sections like:

- possible shared strengths
- possible friction loops
- question worth discussing
- micro-habit to try
- gentle boundary
- visible signals used

Every output must disclose uncertainty and must not imply scientific certainty about attraction, marriage, or long-term success.
