# Compatibility Reflection Contract

## Product Promise

Compatibility reflection is a conversation aid after mutual consent. It is not a prediction, score, ranking, or decision.

Relationship-science grounding supports modest, probabilistic reflection about traits and couple processes. It does not support deterministic pair verdicts, numeric fit claims, or advice to pursue/avoid a specific person.

## Required Inputs

Only use mutually shared or explicitly approved fields:

- values
- visible intent
- visible observance
- lifestyle
- interests
- prompts
- approved share card

## Required Outputs

- `shared_strengths_he`
- `friction_loops`
- `question_to_explore_he`
- `micro_habit_he`
- `gentle_boundary_he`
- `signals_used`
- uncertainty or limit language

## Blocked

Do not use private taste, hidden ranking signals, raw personality scores, raw answers, private messages, exact location, attractiveness, desirability, protected-trait inference, or deterministic compatibility claims.
