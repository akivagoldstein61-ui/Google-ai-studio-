## Scientific Contract

Use the Big Five Aspect Scales as a self-reflection and communication layer, not as a deterministic matching engine. BFAS is a 100-item measure with 10 aspects nested under 5 Big Five domains.

Measurement should be deterministic, auditable, opt-in, and user-owned. LLMs should not score BFAS.

## Big Five Domains And Aspects

Neuroticism:

- volatility,
- withdrawal.

Agreeableness:

- compassion,
- politeness.

Conscientiousness:

- industriousness,
- orderliness.

Extraversion:

- enthusiasm,
- assertiveness.

Openness/Intellect:

- openness,
- intellect.

## Relationship Science Boundaries

Use these as cautious tendencies:

- Neuroticism or low emotional stability is the most consistent personality correlate of lower relationship satisfaction and higher distress/conflict risk.
- Agreeableness and conscientiousness show comparatively consistent positive associations with relationship satisfaction.
- Similarity effects are often smaller and less consistent than folk theories suggest.
- "Opposites attract" should not be treated as a reliable matching principle.

Do not convert these into fate claims, diagnosis, hidden ranking, or compatibility scores.

## Product Patterns

Good outputs:

- reflective profile insights,
- "how I communicate under stress" summaries,
- possible strengths and friction points,
- questions to discuss,
- micro-habits for conflict repair,
- user-controlled sharing cards,
- settings-only self-understanding.

Risky outputs:

- deterministic compatibility percentages,
- hidden ranking boosts,
- "this person is bad for you" claims,
- clinical labels,
- LLM-inferred traits from chat/photos,
- partner scoring without consent.

## Gemini Use

Gemini can help explain user-owned BFAS results, draft reflective copy, suggest communication questions, or produce structured summaries. It should not administer opaque scoring or infer traits from unrelated behavior.

Always use uncertainty markers and user agency language.

## Plugin Integration

Use plugins when personality features move beyond conceptual design:

- GitHub: inspect questionnaire components, scoring code, schemas, settings, and sharing controls.
- Neon Postgres: plan opt-in trait storage, retention, auditability, privacy controls, and user-owned deletion.
- Browser Use: verify consent, editability, visibility controls, and settings-only placement.
- Figma and Canva: design reflection cards, onboarding, sharing surfaces, and explanatory visuals.
- Hugging Face: inspect personality, compatibility, communication-coaching papers, datasets, or evaluation artifacts.
