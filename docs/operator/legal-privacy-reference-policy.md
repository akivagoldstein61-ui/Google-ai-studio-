# Kesher Legal and Privacy Reference Policy

## Purpose

This document defines how Kesher handles legal, privacy, compliance, and policy resources inside the Skills Hub and related app surfaces.

Legal and privacy material must be informational unless a separate legally reviewed product task explicitly changes the surface.

## Core rule

Legal and privacy resources are references, not coaching skills.

Israeli Privacy Law belongs in Legal / Privacy Reference.

It must not be presented as:

- a relationship skill
- a coaching exercise
- a therapy-like prompt
- legal advice
- compliance certification
- personalized legal interpretation

## Required label

Use this label or equivalent copy wherever Israeli Privacy Law or similar legal/privacy material appears:

> Informational privacy and legal reference. Not legal advice. For legal decisions, consult qualified counsel.

## Allowed uses

Legal/privacy references may be used for:

- internal implementation checklists
- operator learning
- privacy education
- data-rights explanation
- retention explanation
- compliance planning
- links to official resources
- legal-review preparation

## Forbidden uses

Legal/privacy references must not:

- include completion or progress states
- include Start, Save, Apply, or skill-practice treatment
- make personalized legal recommendations
- claim legal compliance without review
- advise users how to assert legal rights in a personalized way
- replace qualified legal review
- appear in the interactive deepening roadmap

## Future legal-literacy surface

A future legal-literacy feature is allowed only if all of the following are true:

- separate approved task exists
- legal reviewer is assigned
- scope is informational only
- jurisdiction is explicit
- no personalized legal advice is generated
- copy has disclaimers
- safety/privacy review passes
- app has clear separation from Practice Skills

## Placement model

Legal / Privacy Reference should include:

- Israeli Privacy Law
- privacy rights explanations
- data retention explanations
- account deletion references
- terms and policy explanations
- implementation compliance checklists

Practice Skills should not include legal/privacy references.

## Regression checks

- Israeli Privacy Law is not categorized as Practice Skill.
- Legal/privacy pages have no completion action.
- Legal/privacy pages contain informational-only label.
- Legal/privacy pages do not provide legal advice.
- Legal/privacy content is not included in the interactive deepening backlog.
