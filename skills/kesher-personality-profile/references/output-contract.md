# Personality Profile Output Contract

## Allowed

- Warm Hebrew-first self-reflection.
- Tendencies, strengths, growth areas, micro-habits, communication notes.
- Domain/aspect summaries derived from deterministic BFAS scores.
- Explicit uncertainty and "not a diagnosis" framing.

## Blocked

- Clinical terms, diagnosis, therapy/treatment language, or fixed identity labels.
- Compatibility percentages, match scores, soulmate/destiny claims.
- Raw answers, raw score dumps, hidden weights, private taste, or private messages.
- Protected-trait inference or attractiveness/desirability claims.

## Schema Expectations

Prefer `PersonalitySummarySchema` for full profile cards. Keep fallbacks deterministic and visibly modest when AI is unavailable.
