# Permissioned Share Card Contract

## Share Card Contents

Allowed:

- Short owner-approved personality summary.
- Strengths/watch-outs in non-clinical language.
- Communication preferences or micro-habits.
- Expiry and recipient metadata.

Blocked:

- Raw answers.
- Raw BFAS/aspect scores.
- Hidden ranking signals.
- Private taste profile.
- Private messages.
- Negative labels or compatibility verdicts.

## Lifecycle

1. Draft summary.
2. Owner previews and edits/removes sections.
3. Owner chooses recipient and expiry.
4. Server creates access record.
5. Recipient views within scope.
6. Owner can revoke.
7. Expired/revoked cards deny access.

Revocation copy must say it stops future in-app access, not screenshots or memory.
